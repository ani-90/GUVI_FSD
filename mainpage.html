Welcome to Mouthwaters!!!

I am sure that lot of us cook like it is an experiment(myself included). Well the last 18 months have taught me the importance of mastering the art of cooking, for it is a very important skill and will come handy, if you ever have to travel to some other place and be alone. You need not know how to cook something extravagant, however knowing how to prepare simple items which you consume on a daily basis can prove to be extremely valuable. You also need not depend on someone else to satisfy your appetite.I have realised the importance of being independent and thus in the last 18 months I have learnt to make quite a lot of dishes, with more success than I had ever expected. It gives you immense satisfaction when someone appreciates something which you have done with a lot of effort.

Thus I have developed this website in which I would love to share the steps to prepare food items which will prove to be very useful.
